It was brought here by human hands – meant to serve, to decorate, to be of use. But once it spread out of control, people began to fight against it. Today, goldenrod (Solidago canadensis, Solidago gigantea) is one of the most invasive plants in Poland, becoming a symbol of the unpredictable consequences of human interference with nature. Disturbing the natural balance can ultimately turn against its makers.
